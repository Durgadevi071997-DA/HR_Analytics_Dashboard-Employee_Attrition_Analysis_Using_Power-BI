
##HR-Analytics-Dashboard-Employee-Attrition-Analysis


## Project Objectives

Analyze employee attrition trends.

Identify departments with the highest attrition.

Evaluate employee demographics.

Compare education fields and job roles.

Monitor salary and workforce metrics.

Build an interactive HR dashboard.

Generate business insights for HR decision-making.
## Business Problem

Employee attrition is one of the major challenges faced by organizations. High employee turnover increases recruitment costs, reduces productivity, and affects overall business performance. This project aims to identify the primary factors contributing to employee attrition and provide actionable insights for improving employee retention.

## Business Insights

Research & Development has the largest workforce.

Employee attrition rate is approximately 16.12%.

Employees working overtime are more likely to leave.

Lower job satisfaction contributes to higher attrition.

Salary and career growth significantly influence employee retention.
## Findings

Total Employees: 1,470

Attrition Rate: 16.12%

Active Employees: 1,233

Average Salary: 6.50K

Average Employee Age: 36.92 Years
## Outcome of Project

The dashboard enables HR professionals to monitor workforce trends, identify high-risk employee groups, and support strategic decision-making through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators.
